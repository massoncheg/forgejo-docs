Forgejo is [committed](https://codeberg.org/forgejo/code-of-conduct#our-pledge) to creating an inclusive community where everyone feels welcome and safe.
The well-being of our members is important to us and if anyone feels unsafe or uncomfortable for any reason, we encourage you to reach out to the [Moderation Team](https://codeberg.org/forgejo/governance/src/branch/main/TEAMS.md#moderation).

You can contact the Moderation Team via email at `moderation@forgejo.org` or via matrix at `@forgejo-moderation:matrix.org`.
Your conversations will remain confidential and are only disclosed in accordance with our [moderation process](https://codeberg.org/forgejo/governance/src/branch/main/MODERATION-PROCESS.md).

The Moderation Team is there to listen to your concerns and will do its best to find the best solutions, ensuring that everyone feels safe and respected in our community.
Actions taken are [documented](https://codeberg.org/forgejo/governance/issues?labels=106732&state=all) in an anonymized way within the governance repository, ensuring transparency and accountability.
The team is committed to addressing any behavior that goes against the [Code of Conduct](https://codeberg.org/forgejo/code-of-conduct) or the law.

Together, we can foster a positive and inclusive environment for all Forgejo community members.
